ESCaPe 2023


Engineering Students Conference at Peradeniya 2023

23 Jan 2023 - EOE Pereira Theatre, Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya.


Overview

EscaPe is the annual project symposium of the Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya. It presents you with the research projects of the undergraduates of the Department of Computer Engineering. ESCaPe 2023 is the 7th symposium that is organized by the department and this time the symposium is open for a broader audience and aims to build a platform for the undergraduates to present their research ideas to the industry and academic community.
