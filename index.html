Overview

EscaPe is the annual project symposium of the Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya. It presents you with the research projects of the undergraduates of the Department of Computer Engineering. ESCaPe 2021 is the 6th symposium that is organized by the department and this time the symposium is open for a broader audience and aims to build a platform for the undergraduates to present their research ideas to the industry and academic community.

Accelerated AI

Artificial Intelligence has now become a mainstream technology in real-world applications and large-scale investments. AI offers solutions and opportunities for organizations across all sectors which can accelerate business processes, simplify IT operations and uncover new insights etc. Every application of AI is attracting the tech industry and ensuring excellent performance. The main facets which are accelerating the rate of innovation within the area of Artificial Intelligence are Advanced computing architecture, Progress in Deep Neural Networks and Data Analysis performance. Current research and the efforts in various fields are advancing the innovation in artificial intelligence and large-scale computing. These trends will make AI technology the most sought-after technology in the market and AI will reach its peak in near future.

Data Driven AI

Artificial Intelligence, especially with machine learning, deep learning and advanced analytics can automate decision making and insight gathering in the data-driven world. Identifying patterns within data, based on mathematical and statistical models and making accurate assumptions over them is very important in modern science and those results are useful for many fields. By today, Data Driven AI has amplified the value of data many times over. Data Driven AI is becoming more and more a part of the activities traditionally covered by the engineering data analysis and simulation community. Recent improvements in the application of AI, machine learning and deep learning, have brought these technologies to the fore in every area of industry.

Keynote speakers

Mr. Jeevan Gnanam

Founder / Group CEO of VeracityAI

Mr.Jeevan Gnanam is the founder and Group CEO of VeracityAI. He is a data science & AI enthusiast, an entrepreneurial idea generator, a mentor, and a facilitator for entrepreneurial development for youth in Sri Lanka. He is the Chief Executive Officer and Executive Director at St.Anthony's Industries Group which is one of the oldest and largest conglomerates for manufacturing textiles, building materials, plastic products, industrial fasteners, cement, fiber cement and corrugated roofing sheets.

Mr.Jeevan Gnanam also serves as the CEO of Anton which is one of the largest plastic manufacturers in the country. His commitment has led Anton to many firsts in plastics manufacturing in Sri Lanka. Mr. Jeevan is also a co-founder of Providence which has now grown to include Providence BPO.

He is a founder member of the Lanka Angel Network, which is targeting to support Sri Lankan entrepreneurs and companies to grow by providing venture capital and mentorship.

He is also the immediate past chairman of SLASSCOM, which is the national chamber for the IT/BPM industry in Sri Lanka. Mr. Jeevan is the visionary behind Sri Lanka's first premier operational IT park, Orion City and his leadership encompasses HATCH Sri Lanka, SAKS, OrionStellar.


Mr. Thushera Kawdawatta

CEO of Axiata Digital Labs

Mr. Thushera Kawdawatta is the Chief Executive Officer at Axiata Digital Labs which is an innovative software service provider, offering telco-focused digital and IT services and solutions through successful digital transformation.

He is a tech enthusiast leader with accountability and responsibility in software delivery quality and specialized in Software architecture, Engineering management, and process improvement.

Over 24 years of experience in the industry have led him to be a people-oriented leader who thoroughly enjoys his work. Initially, he spent over 19 years at Virtusa as the Chief Software Architect contributing to software product development. During his years, Virtusa won the 2012 WITSA Global ICT Excellence Award for its Àkura School Management System. And also he was awarded for best Productivity software of the year 1999.

With his extensive experience, Mr. Thushera Kawdawatta has been working on over 100 software projects across numerous reputed global enterprises over the past few years while launching many IT initiatives. Mr. Thushera was appointed as a board member of CSSL, and a member of the parliament select committee on AI standards of Sri Lanka.
